Shipping Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Domestic Shipping (Pakistan)

  • Standard delivery: 3 to 5 business days.
  • Remote areas: 5 to 8 business days.
  • Free shipping on orders above PKR 5,000.

International Shipping

  • Estimated delivery: 7 to 14 business days.
  • Delivery time may vary due to customs and local carriers.
  • Custom duties/taxes are paid by the customer where applicable.

Order Processing

  • Orders are processed Monday to Saturday.
  • Cut-off time: 2:00 PM PKT.
  • Orders placed after cut-off are processed next business day.
Tracking details are shared by SMS/email as soon as the parcel is dispatched.
