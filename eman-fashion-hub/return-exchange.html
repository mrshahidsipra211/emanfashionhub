Eligibility

  • Request within 7 days of delivery.

How to Request

  • Contact support with order ID and issue details.
  • Share clear photos for damaged/incorrect items.
  • Our team confirms pickup or drop-off instructions.

Refund Timeline

  • Quality check: 1 to 2 business days after receiving item.
